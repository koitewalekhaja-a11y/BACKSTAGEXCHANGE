WHO WE ARE

We Are Event Planner & Organizer Since 2015

We are the most effective and most identifiable event management company in Goa making management easier at all levels, beginning from the very first step and always reaching the end.

Connect With Us

Mission & Vision

We aim to be the most professional event agency in Goa by offering planned coordination, smooth execution and work with finesse. We make sure that it isn’t just about delivering a product, but it is also about offering guidance & service to a client before and after an event.

Our Mission

Goa is a massive tourist destination and the number of events that take place in Goa keeps escalating year after year. The scope & interest for event management in Goa is high which helps us work towards our goal of creating employment for the youth, building the event sector and boosting tourism.

Our Vision

By the year 2020, we aim to achieve expansion in major states and provide top quality events all over the country.
